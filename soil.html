SOIL POLUTION

Soil contamination or soil pollution as part of land deggradation is caused by the presence of
antibiotics(human-made) chemicals orother alternation in the nature soil enviornment.


(I) Causes of Soil Pollution:

  • Industrial waste.
  • Deforestation.
  • Excessive use of fertilizers and pesticides.
  • Garbage Pollution.
    •

    (II) Effects of Soil Pollution:

  • Climate change,
  • Loss of soil Contamination.
  • Water Sources Contamination.
  • Toxic soil harmful impact on ecosystem and biodiversity.
    •

    (III) Prevention of Soil Pollution:

  • Reforestation.
  • Controlled farming practices.
  • Reduce,Recycle,and Reuse.
  • Use biodigradable products.
  • Reduce the use of pestisides and fertilizers.
