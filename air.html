AIR POLLUTION

Air pollution refers to may physical, chemical and biological change in the air. It is the contamination of air by harmful gasses dust and smoke which effects the plants, animals & human.


Causes of Air Pollution:

  • Industries are major contributor to air pollution.
  • Vehicle Emissions.
  • Household and framing chemical.
  • Smoking.
  • Forest Fire.
    •

    Effects of Air Pollution:

  • Global Warming.
  • Climate Change.
  • Acid Rain.
  • Smog effect.
  • Respiratry health problems.
  • Extinction of animal species.
    •

    Prevention of Air Pollution:

  • Reduce the uses of fossil fuels.
  • Avoid the burning leaves, plastic and others.
  • Avoid uses of harmful gasses.
