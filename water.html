WATER POLUTION

Water pollution is the contanmination of water bodies, usually as a result of human activities.
Water bodies include for example lakes,rivers,oceans,aquifers and groundwater.


Causes of Water Pollution:

  • Discharge of domestic & industrial wastes.
  • Leakage from contaminated water tanks.
  • Radioactive waste and atmospheric deposition.
  • River dumping.
  • Forest Fire.
    •

    Effects of Water Pollution:

  • Degradation of aquatic ecosystem.
  • Lack of pure drinking water.
  • Many water bore diseases are caused.
  • Lack of water for irrgation.
    •

    Prevention of Water Pollution:

  • Stop dumping garbage in water.
  • Wastewater treatment plant.
  • Stop putting industrial chemical and garbage in water.
  • Reduce your plastic consumption.
